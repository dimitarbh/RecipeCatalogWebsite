5 minutes recipe

This recipe is so easy and quick, that quite frankly I’m embarrassed to be calling

it a recipe at all. Be warned, a 5-minute masala sandwich can be utterly addictive and completely deceiving in its simplicity!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white sandwich bread
  • About 1.5 tsp dairy free-margarine or plant-based butter
  • 1 small Persian or baby cucumber or about 1/5th of a large Cucumber
  • 1 large ripe firm tomato
  • 1⁄3 a small red onion (or substitute a regular white onion)
  • 1⁄4 tsp chaat masala
  • frozen french fries
